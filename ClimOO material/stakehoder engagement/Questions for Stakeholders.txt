Questions for StakeholdersThe following list presents the questions used to drive the initial discussions held with stakeholders to define ClimOO OntologyŐs domain of interest and Competency Questions (CQs). They are organized in subsets pertaining to sustainability, agriculture, plastic pollution, and climate change, to facilitate readability. 
Sustainability and the systems of reference
- What does sustainability mean to you, especially with regard to plastics, climate change, and agriculture?- What are your frameworks of reference with regard to sustainability, climate change, and plastics?- How do you delimit an agricultural system, especially with regard to South Tyrol? Which system scales and levels do we need to analyze the system(s) of interest?- What does a sustainable agricultural system look like? And in South Tyrol?- Who does sustainability concern for you? Who will be/are the stakeholders of the ClimOO project?
Plastics and microplastics
- What is your definition of plastics? And microplastics? What are the characteristics that you primarily refer to plastics?- What are the main types of plastics and microplastics, especially with regard to agriculture? - According to Hartmann et al. (2019), we should also consider as microplastic debris some litters that are not plastics in an intrinsic sense (according to Ňmaterial science viewÓ), like, for instance, some inorganic polymer-based materials, some elastomers, some fibers, etc. Do you agree with that?- What are the functions that plastics fulfill in agriculture? And the usages? Which ones are the most relevant and should be prioritized in our model? - Hartmann et al. (2019) suggest defining microplastic size between 1 and 1000 µm. Many works instead use 5mm as a threshold. Which one do you suggest using?- What are the primary processes in which plastics are involved (in agriculture)? - Can we consider all plastics degradable at least at a certain level? If so, how to deal with the persistence of microplastics and, in general, debris? How to treat biodegradable plastics in this regard?- Do we also need to represent plastic polymers? What is the level of detail that we need in our model regarding plastics and microplastics? Do we need to represent pollutant microplastic adsorption/absorption?- Additives: Shall we cover them in our ontology? If so, which level of detail do we need for representing plastic additives? Is it enough of a general representation of additives? Do we need to define specific types of additives? In case which are the most relevant ones?
Plastics, climate change, and agriculture- Which features do you consider the most relevant with regard to plastic pollution? And climate change?- What are the primary consequences of plastic pollution in agriculture? - What are the primary consequences of climate change in agriculture? - What is the level of detail that the ClimOO ontology should cover concerning climate change?- How do plastics and microplastics contribute to climate change? - How does climate change contribute to (micro-)plastic degradation and microplastic pollution?- What are the main connections between plastics, microplastic pollution, and sustainability in agriculture?- What are the relevant sustainability issues concerning plastic pollution and climate change in - agriculture? And regarding South Tyrol? - Which are those to prioritize?
Plastic pollution analysis 
- Do we need to represent experiments and methods of measurement? If so, in which details and which experiments and methods? What are the purposes?- Do we need to refer to experiment tools, procedures, etc?Sustainability goals and assessments- Do we need to set some sustainability objectives to achieve? If so, which ones? Any frameworks of reference?- Which indicators shall we cover, if any? Any frameworks of reference?- Do we need to refer to some environmental performance instruments? If so, which ones? Which degree of detail should their descriptions have?Data- What types of data do you need to model? - What are the purposes? - For whom do you need to model them?Bibliography
