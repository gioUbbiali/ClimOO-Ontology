# ClimOO Ontology Suite


## Description

This repository maintains the ClimOO Ontology suite. ClimOO suite is a domain reference architecture for plastics, (micro-)plastic pollution, and related interrelations with climate change, especially within agricultural systems. Use case: local South Tyrolean (Italy) agricultural production. 

The ClimOO Ontology suite is composed of four nested modules, namely:

- [Plastics ontology (PlasticO)](https://w3id.org/climoo/climoo-plastico) extends [Sustainability Core Ontology (SCO)](https://github.com/gioUbbiali/Sustainability-Core-Ontology) and [Basic Formal Ontology (BFO)](https://github.com/BFO-ontology/BFO-2020) to represent polymer molecules, plastic materials, pieces of plastic and plastic objects, along with corresponding life cycles and pollution processes. Emphasis is also dedicated to relevant assessment devices and related data, especially with regard to sustainability.
- ClimOO-SDC2 forks and updates the [Sustainable Development and Climate Ontology (SDC Onto)](https://github.com/hbabaie1/Sustainable-Development-and-Climate-SDC-ontology), providing a model for climate and climate change (main focus on environmental features and related interconnections). ClimOO-SDC2 extends [Sustainability Core Ontology (SCO)](https://github.com/gioUbbiali/Sustainability-Core-Ontology) and [Basic Formal Ontology (BFO)](https://github.com/BFO-ontology/BFO-2020).
- Plastics Interconnecting with Climate Change Ontology (PICCO) extends PlasticO and ClimOO-SDC2 representations to interrelations between (micro-)plastic pollution and climate change.
- PICCOmela applies PICCO representation to agriculture systems, especially apple production.


The ClimOO ontology suite has been developed as part of the "Climate, Plastics and Sustainability: Ontology and Operationalisation (ClimOO)" project. See the [ClimOO project website](https://www.pthsta.it/en/research/climate-plastics-and-sustainability).


## Material

Currently, this repository hosts the initial working progress version of the ClimOO Ontology suite, ClimOO V1.0.0. ClimOO V1.0.0 is formalized in [Web Ontology Language (OWL)](https://www.w3.org/TR/owl2-overview/) and covers English as natural language. ClimOO V1.0.0 conforms to [OBO-Foundry principles](https://obofoundry.org/principles/fp-000-summary.html).
